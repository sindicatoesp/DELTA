---
- id: P0-1
  title: "Fix NODE_ENV in Dockerfile"
  file: Dockerfile.app
  line: 30
  root_cause: >
    Dockerfile.app sets ENV NODE_ENV=development at line 30, which runs the
    production container in development mode at runtime. Although the build step
    (RUN yarn build at line 21) compiles a production bundle, the runtime
    process.env.NODE_ENV is "development", disabling Node.js production
    optimizations, potentially enabling debug-friendly error messages, and
    violating the assumption that NODE_ENV=production in deployed containers.
  fix_complexity: Trivial
  requires_spec: false
  requires_test: false
  fix_summary: "Change ENV NODE_ENV=development to ENV NODE_ENV=production in Dockerfile.app."
  antipattern: null
---
