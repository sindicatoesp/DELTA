---
- id: P0-13
  title: "Fix rejectUnauthorized: false in SMTP transport"
  file: app/utils/email.ts
  line: 25
  root_cause: >
    The SMTP transport created in createTransporter() (app/utils/email.ts) sets
    `rejectUnauthorized: false` in the tls options at line 25. The comment reads
    "Debugging, remove this if working fine." With this flag set, the SMTP client
    accepts any TLS certificate including self-signed and expired ones, making
    the connection vulnerable to man-in-the-middle attacks. All outgoing emails
    (password resets, invitations, notifications) pass through this transport.
    The line 23 sets `minVersion: "TLSv1.2"` which is correct but ineffective
    without certificate verification.
  fix_complexity: Trivial
  requires_spec: false
  requires_test: false
  fix_summary: >
    Remove the `rejectUnauthorized: false` line (or change it to true) in the
    tls block of the SMTP transport options in app/utils/email.ts.
  antipattern: AP-10
---
